Slices Of Life

Slices Of Life is a fun and whimsical peek into to a diverse set of people, cultures, colourful lives, hobbies and even short stories told through the simple question of "What's Your Favourite Sandwich?" The project takes the form of an interactive and immersive installation in the style of a classic American Sandwich Deli, complete with designed ingredient labels, packages - all taking the viewer through a series of sandwich combinations learned from real interviews all while providing the ability to contribute to the collection through a DIY rubber stamping station.
