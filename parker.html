Behind the Ink

An exploration of the history and meanings behind tattoos. 'Behind the Ink' explores why people get tattoos in the modern day as well as the origin of both tattoos and the stigma surrounding them in the UK. It aims to remove these stigmas and embrace the unique and individual art people have chosen to attach to their bodies forever. It consists of a publication detailing a range of information about tattoos as well as a set of AR posters visually showing the meaning behind the ink.
