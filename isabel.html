The Art of Interacting Incorrectly

The Art of Interacting Incorrectly challenges our seamless interactions with technology by subverting how it can be used. Through the creation of an animation using unconventional methods, along with an accompanying website, the project explores how disrupting technology can encourage more thoughtful and engaged interactions. By taking familiar aspects of using a laptop and presenting them in unfamiliar ways, it also aims to reintroduce a sense of novelty to our experience with everyday digital tools.
