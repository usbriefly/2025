Unplugged Reality

Unplugged Reality is a project on how live music and concerts can be a form of escapism from the real world. Individuals experience comfort and joy within a shared space, and this project aims to visually capture the emotional resonance and communal spirit of these events. Unplugged Reality delves into the psychological benefits of attending a concert, and encourages others to do so. Key themes explored within this project have been anticipation of the show, audience interaction with the band/artist, atmosphere, community, and the performance itself. Concerts provide vital moments of human connection and emotional well-being, and it's important to maintain this in such a digital world that we live in now.
