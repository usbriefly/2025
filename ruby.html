Sound. Can you see it?

My project is driven by an interest with synesthesia and the relationship between sound and vision, exploring how the interaction of these senses can influence design. With a strong interest in sound design, I became intrigued by the idea of visualising sound. Using typography as a medium, I sought to experiment with how text can be transformed to represent auditory experiences. Alongside text manipulation, I was also interested in exploring how we perceive sound within design. By examining the interplay of space, shape, and time, my work encourages people to think about sound beyond just hearing it.This project serves as both an artistic exploration and an extension of my research.
