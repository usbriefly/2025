Miss xxxx

Miss xxxx , is an exploration of memory, anonymity, and human connection. Centred around a collection of found handwritten letters, the project invites the viewer into an intimate conversation with someone they will never know. Emotion, routine, and the details of a stranger's everyday life, the project seeks to explore and re-contextualise the mundane through a series of letterpress prints.
